Projects

Project-1: TEXT SUMMARIZATION

Text Summarizer is a user-friendly web application using Deep Learning Concepts. It generates concise summaries of uploaded documents, simplifying information retrieval for users. Text summarization condenses text to its core meaning, capturing main ideas without specific details. Techniques include extractive methods (combining existing sentences) and abstractive methods (generating new sentences). Summarization is used in news, document, and research paper summaries, enabling quick understanding without reading the entire text.

Project-2: SKIN CANCER DETECTION

Skin cancer Detection web application utilizes machine learning and computer vision techniques to detect skin cancer from images. Users can upload pictures of skin lesions or moles, and the application will analyze the images to determine if they show signs of skin cancer or other dermatological conditions. The application can provide users with instant feedback on the likelihood of the lesion being cancerous, along with additional information and recommendations for further medical consultation.
